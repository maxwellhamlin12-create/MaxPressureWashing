HOW TO ADD YOUR REAL PHOTOS
===========================

1. Take a BEFORE and an AFTER photo of the same spot, from the same angle.
   Landscape (wide) photos work best. Phone photos are fine.

2. Drop them into this "images" folder with simple names, for example:
     driveway-before.jpg
     driveway-after.jpg

3. Open index.html and find the matching slider (look for the comments
   that say "BEFORE/AFTER SLIDER"). Delete the placeholder <div class="ph...">
   block and un-comment the <img> tag right below it, updating the file name.

4. Do the same for the "PHOTO OF MAX PLACEHOLDER" in the Meet Max section —
   a friendly photo of Max with his gear works great there.

Tip: keep photos under ~500 KB each so the site stays fast. Screenshots or
exports from the Photos app at "Large" size are usually perfect.
