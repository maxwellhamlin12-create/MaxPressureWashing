FAQ

Good questions

How do I get a quote?

Walkways, patios, and driveways have set prices — they're listed right on this page. For bigger or combined jobs, call or text (716) 329-2681 or use the form below, and I'll give you a free quote, usually the same day.

What surfaces do you clean?

Concrete, pavers, and stone: driveways, walkways, patios, and sidewalks. I keep the work at ground level and use the right pressure for each surface, so delicate pavers get a more gentle wash than a concrete driveway.

Do you bring your own equipment and water?

I bring the pressure washer and all the gear. Like most pressure washing services, I hook up to your outdoor spigot for water — a typical job uses about as much as watering the lawn.

Do I need to be home during the job?

Nope — as long as the outdoor spigot is on and cars are off the surface being washed, I can do the whole job while you're out, then send you the after photos.

How do I book?

Call or text (716) 329-2681, email maxwellhamlin12@gmail.com, or fill out the form below. I'll confirm a day and time that works for you — evenings and weekends included.
